Modular blackletter typeface inspired by scripts used throughout Western Europe from approximately 1150
to well into the 17th century. Fraktur, Schawabacher, Rotunda were references used for the development.
Kunt is designed to be used in short text, titles, banners, cover books, charts, editorials, branding.
